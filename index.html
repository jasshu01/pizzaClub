Pizza

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round,flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc.), which isthen baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta.
